The main chemical in tobacco is nicotine which is highly addictive.

Research has found that just a few cigarettes can alter your brain chemistry enough to make you crave tobacco.

Research on rat brains can teach us about the effects of nicotine on the human brain. The red areas show the parts of the brain that react to nicotine. These images show just how quickly the brain becomes sensitive to nicotine, making addiction more likely after just a few doses.
