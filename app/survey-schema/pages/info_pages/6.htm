If a pregnant girl uses alcohol, marijuana, or other drugs, she puts her baby at risk.

Exposure to substances before birth can:

  • Harm the baby's developing brain and central nervous system
  • Cause permanent defects to the body's major organs
  • Cause miscarriage, stunted fetal growth, fetal death, premature birth, addiction in the newborn baby, and learning and behavioral problems during childhood.
