Like alcohol, marijuana impairs driving ability and can cause fatal motor vehicle accidents.

Scientific studies show that marijuana use increases the odds of unsafe driving by 30%.

  • Marijuana affects concentration, perception, coordination and reaction time that can last up to 24 hours after smoking.
  • Marijuana can negatively impact a driver’s attentiveness, perception of time and speed, and the ability to draw on information obtained through past experiences.
