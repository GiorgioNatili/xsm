If a girl becomes pregnant, alcohol and drug use can hurt the baby.

When a girl who is pregnant uses alcohol or drugs, so does the baby. See a normal six week old's brain.

  • Is harmful to the baby's development.
  • Can cause permanent defects to the major organs and central nervous system.
  • Can cause preterm birth, miscarriage, fetal death, stunted fetal growth, addiction in the newborn baby, and learning and behavioral problems during childhood.
