Drinking alcohol causes accidents.

Accidents are the leading cause of death among young people.

  • Motor vehicle crashes are the leading cause of death among youth ages 15 to 20 years.
  • 16,000 people are killed annually due to drunk driving and drugged living (National Highway Traffic Safety Administration).

Alcohol

  • All states have a zero-tolerance law for anyone under the age of 21, which means you lose your license if caught drinking and driving
  • Alcohol decreases coordination, slows reflexes, distorts vision
  • Drivers who have been drinking are 6x more likely to cause an accident.
  • Among teens, 46% of all motor vehicle fatalities involve alcohol.
