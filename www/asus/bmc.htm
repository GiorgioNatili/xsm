Welcome to the Adolescent Substance Use Screening program, and thanks for helping with this important research study.

This program will ask you about your use of tobacco, alcohol, and drugs.

We will keep your answers to these questions confidential and they will not be put in your medical record. We will not tell your parents how you answered the questions unless we become worried about your safety, as we described in the study consent form.

It will take about five minutes to answer all the questions in this program. Please answer every question by tapping your choice. Read each question carefully before you make a choice. There is no "BACK" button to go back and change your answer. Touch the "NEXT" button to go to the next question.

If you need help with anything, ask the study assistant.
