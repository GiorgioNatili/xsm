The main chemical in tobacco is nicotine. Nicotine is highly addictive.

Research has found that just a few cigarettes can change your brain enough to make you crave tobacco

Researchers study rat brains to model what happens to the human brain. The red areas show the brain parts that react to nicotine. This shows that the brain quickly becomes sensitive to nicotine, making addiction more likely after just a few doses.
