Take your prescriptions exactly as your doctor says.

Never share your medicine or take someone else’s.

  • Prescription drugs, like pain-killers, stimulants, and tranquilizers are safe when used as directed by your doctor.
  • Prescription drugs are now the second most commonly abused drug among teens, after marijuana.

    • When abused:

  • Pain killers like OxyContinTM (OC’s) or Vicodin can cause addiction, brain damage, and sudden death.
  • Stimulant medications like Ritalin, Adderall, and Dexedrine can cause addiction, seizures, rapid heart beat, and sudden death.
  • Tranquilizers like Klonopin or Xanax can cause addiction, drowsiness, accidents, and death from respiratory depression.
  • Dextromethorpan (DXM), an ingredient commonly found in cough medicine can cause rapid heart beat, nausea, urinary retention, and psychosis.
