Areas of the brain affected by drugs and alcohol
Prefrontal Cortex: Drugs and alcohol interfere with making wise decisions.
Motor Cortex: Drugs and alcohol can cause a stroke leading to paralysis.
Reward Pathway: Gets hijacked by drugs so you only feel good when you are on drugs, and you keep craving more.
Hippocampus: Drugs and alcohol cause short-term memory loss.
Cerrebelum: Drugs and alcohol impair balance and coordination, affect emotions.
