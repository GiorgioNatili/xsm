Alcohol and marijuana both impair driving, leading to many car crashes.

Car crashes are the leading cause of death among 15 to 20 year olds.

    40% of teens that died in motor vehicle crashes tested positive for alcohol, and 20% tested positive for marijuana.


    Marijuana can negatively affect a driver's judgement, attentiveness, coordination, reaction time, and perception of speed.


    The effects of Marijuana can last 24 hours or more AFTER smoking.
