Substance use and sexual risk

Many research studies show that people who drink heavily or use drugs have a much higher rate of:

  • Having unprotected sex
  • Getting HIV or some other sexually-transmitted infection
  • Unplanned pregnancies and (for women) abortion

Most rapes involve alcohol, and often the victim is too drunk to resist.
