Marijuana use damages your hippocampus, hurting your ability to remember and learn things.



A large research study that tracked people from birth to age 38 found that those who started using marijuana at least ONCE A WEEK before age 18 had a significant drop in their IQ, while no drop in IQ was seen for those who did not use marijuana.
